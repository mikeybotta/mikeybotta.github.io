About Me

The short version of it...

My name is Mikey, and I'm 23 years old. I am currently living in the beautiful city of Austin, Texas. As someone who loves the outdoors, warm weather, and good food, ATX has proven itself to be an incredible choice. I have a Mini Aussiedoodle named Skye, who is just over a year old, although I doubt her puppy energy is ever going to fade. I really love pop music, especially (right now) Zara Larsson, Madison Beer, and Taylor Swift. I work as a tech recruiter at Austin Fraser, a pretty small recruiting agency based here in Austin. And that about wraps me up in a nutshell, but read on for an expansive journey through my life, my career, and my future.

My journey...

it all started in new jersey

I was raised in a small town in northern New Jersey. Whereas the initial impression of NJ for many is of congested suburbs and the stinky Newark airport, my hometown, Sparta, is set up on a mountain, surrounded by woods, with clean air and bright stars in the night sky.
